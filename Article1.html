Covid-19 Update

The latest news regarding the Covid-19 pandemic

As the Covid-19 pandemic continues, More and more people are at risk of, or have caught the virus. It has been confirmed that both the US and Spains death tolls have also exceeded that of China, along with the already confirmed Italy, where the virus has had the worst effect so far. despite the lower death toll however, it is confirmed that the US has the most cases of the virus at over 160,000. This is in comparison to Italys 101,000 and Spains 87,000. The effects of the virus are beginning to subside in China, which has roughly 82,000 cases, and 3,300 deaths.

As the number of cases of Covid-19 around the world continue to rise, world health organisations are doing everything they can to help stop the spread of the virus, and encourage everyone to play their part in containing the virus by continuing to practice social distancing and by ensuring that they stay clean, in order to ensure that the virus is stopped as quickly as possible.
